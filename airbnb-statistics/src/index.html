Several Airbnb hosts manage more than one listing. A host might offer various rooms within a single apartment, or have several apartments or houses up for rent in their entirety.

Hosts who have numerous listings tend to operate as businesses, are probably not residing in the properties themselves, and often breach regulations established for short-term rentals that aim to preserve residential housing.
