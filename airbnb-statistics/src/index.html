Airbnb for Investors

Room Type

Airbnb providers have the option to offer a range of accommodations including entire houses/apartments, private or shared spaces, and, more recently, hotel rooms. The nature and type of the listed space can vary; for instance, a residential Airbnb property might resemble a hotel in its operations, potentially causing disturbances to neighbors, reducing available housing, and in some cases, breaching legal regulations.

Licenses

Legislation mandates that certain short-term rental properties must have a permit. The chart simplifies the distribution of properties according to their licensing status, categorizing them as licensed, unlicensed, pending, or exempt.

Listings per Host

Several Airbnb hosts manage more than one listing. A host might offer various rooms within a single apartment, or have several apartments or houses up for rent in their entirety. Hosts who have numerous listings tend to operate as businesses, are probably not residing in the properties themselves, and often breach regulations established for short-term rentals that aim to preserve residential housing.
